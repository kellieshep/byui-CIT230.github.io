Springfield Balloon Festival

Springfield had a wonderful experience for all present to experience. A beautiful balloon festival with 15 balloons of varying sizes ans colors with a amazing chance to ride in and get the real ballooning experience.
We had available teethered balloon rides along with a few unteethered experiences available. Prices from $20 a person and up depending on the experience that you wanted. There was also a candle buring at night to see the power and heat that the burners produce with a concert with several live bands free to the public.
