Greenville Hiking Trails Find Beauty

Many people may be surprised to find that Greenville is rich with hiking trails that offer an amazing amount of diversified beauty. We having many different things to find in our small corner of the world.
One of the great attractions, that many are unaware of, is the arch that is just a short drive and hike out of town. We have a natural arch that rivals even those in more traveled places of the nation. We are holding a opportunity to go and visit the arch with guides that will go with you and teach you about all the wonderful other wonders that will surround you. Bring your lunch and meet at the Museum at 10 a.m. for this once in a lifetime opportunity.
