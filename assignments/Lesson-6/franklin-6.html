Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin made a record today with the largest count of ducks migrating south this year. The clean up the children did inconjunction with the oil companies made all the difference from ten years ago. There are many people to thank for this wonderful change in our community. I hope that it continues to improve and that we can have a healthy flock of ducks coming every year.
